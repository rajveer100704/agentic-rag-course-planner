TITLE: State University Course Catalog — Mathematics Courses (Relevant to CS)
ACCESSED: 2025-01-15
INSTITUTION: State University, Department of Mathematics

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 110 — Precalculus
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Satisfactory placement score OR MATH 095 with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Functions, polynomial and rational functions, exponential and logarithmic
functions, trigonometry, and an introduction to sequences and series.
Offered: Fall, Spring, Summer
Grade Requirement to Progress: C or higher to enroll in MATH 120.

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 120 — Calculus I
================================================================================
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: MATH 110 with a grade of C or higher, OR satisfactory calculus
placement score.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Limits, derivatives, integrals, the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus, and
applications.
Offered: Fall, Spring, Summer
Grade Requirement to Progress: C or higher to enroll in MATH 121.

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 121 — Calculus II
================================================================================
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: MATH 120 with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Techniques of integration, series, sequences, parametric curves, and polar
coordinates.
Offered: Fall, Spring

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 215 — Calculus III (Multivariable)
================================================================================
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: MATH 121 with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Partial derivatives, multiple integrals, vector calculus, Green's and Stokes'
theorems.
Offered: Fall, Spring

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 230 — Linear Algebra
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: MATH 120 with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Systems of equations, matrix operations, determinants, vector spaces,
eigenvalues, and linear transformations.
Offered: Fall, Spring

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 240 — Probability and Statistics
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: MATH 120 with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Probability spaces, random variables, distributions, hypothesis testing,
confidence intervals, and linear regression.
Offered: Fall, Spring

================================================================================
SECTION: MATH 300 — Introduction to Proofs
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: MATH 121 with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Logic, set theory, proof techniques (direct, contradiction, induction), and
introduction to abstract mathematics.
Offered: Fall, Spring

TITLE: State University Course Catalog — Data Science Courses
ACCESSED: 2025-01-15
INSTITUTION: State University, Data Science Program

================================================================================
SECTION: DS 101 — Introduction to Data Science
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None
Co-requisites: CS 101 (may be taken concurrently)
Description:
Overview of data science as a field, the data pipeline, basic tools (Jupyter,
pandas), and data ethics. Designed for students exploring the major.
Offered: Fall, Spring

================================================================================
SECTION: DS 201 — Data Wrangling and Visualization
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: DS 101 OR CS 230, AND MATH 110 or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Advanced data cleaning, transformation, and visualization using pandas, seaborn,
and Plotly. Students work with real-world messy datasets.
Offered: Fall, Spring

================================================================================
SECTION: DS 301 — Applied Machine Learning
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: DS 201 with a grade of C or higher, AND CS 220 (Statistics for
Data Science) with a grade of C or higher, AND CS 260 (Linear Algebra for ML)
with a grade of C or higher.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
End-to-end machine learning projects: feature engineering, model selection,
hyperparameter tuning, deployment. Kaggle competitions required.
Offered: Fall only

================================================================================
SECTION: DS 401 — Capstone in Data Science
================================================================================
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: DS 301 with a grade of C or higher, AND completion of at least
90 credit hours, AND senior standing.
Co-requisites: None
Description:
Year-long industry-partnered data science project. Students present findings
to an external panel.
Offered: Spring only
