TITLE: State University — Minor in Data Science: Requirements
ACCESSED: 2025-01-15
INSTITUTION: State University, Data Science Program

================================================================================
SECTION: Minor in Data Science — Overview
================================================================================
Total Credits Required: 18 credit hours
This minor is open to students in any major. No more than 6 credits used to
satisfy your primary major's requirements may also count toward the minor
(this is the double-counting limit).

================================================================================
SECTION: Minor in Data Science — Required Courses
================================================================================
The following are REQUIRED (9 credits):
  • DS 101 — Introduction to Data Science (3 cr)
  • DS 201 — Data Wrangling and Visualization (3 cr)
  • CS 220 — Statistics for Data Science (3 cr)

================================================================================
SECTION: Minor in Data Science — Elective Courses
================================================================================
Choose 9 additional credits from the following:
  • CS 230 — Introduction to Data Science (3 cr)
  • CS 260 — Linear Algebra for Machine Learning (3 cr)
  • CS 320 — Machine Learning (3 cr)
  • DS 301 — Applied Machine Learning (3 cr)
  • CS 430 — Natural Language Processing (3 cr)
  • MATH 240 — Probability and Statistics (3 cr)

Note: At most 6 of the 9 elective credits may be from 200-level courses.
At least 3 of the 9 elective credits must be at the 300-level or above.

================================================================================
SECTION: Minor in Data Science — GPA Requirement
================================================================================
A minimum GPA of 2.5 in all minor courses is required for the minor to appear
on the transcript. Students below 2.5 may petition for an exception.

TITLE: State University — Certificate in Cybersecurity
ACCESSED: 2025-01-15
INSTITUTION: State University, Department of Computer Science

================================================================================
SECTION: Certificate in Cybersecurity — Overview
================================================================================
Total Credits Required: 15 credit hours
The Cybersecurity Certificate is a standalone credential. All courses must be
completed with a C or higher. Certificate students are not required to be
degree-seeking students at State University, but must be admitted as certificate
students or as degree-seeking students.

================================================================================
SECTION: Certificate in Cybersecurity — Required Courses
================================================================================
  • CS 101 — Introduction to Programming (3 cr)  [or equivalent, with advisor approval]
  • CS 210 — Computer Systems and Organization (3 cr)
  • CS 240 — Introduction to Cybersecurity (3 cr)
  • CS 440 — Advanced Cybersecurity (3 cr)
  • CS 311 — Operating Systems (3 cr)

Note: These courses have their own prerequisites. Students must satisfy prerequisites
to enroll. No prerequisite waivers are granted for certificate students unless
individually approved by the CS Department Chair.

TITLE: State University — B.S. in Data Science: Degree Requirements
ACCESSED: 2025-01-15
INSTITUTION: State University, Data Science Program

================================================================================
SECTION: B.S. in Data Science — Overview
================================================================================
Total Credits Required: 120 credit hours
Minimum GPA: 2.0 cumulative; 2.5 GPA in DS-prefix courses required to graduate.
This major is housed in the joint CS/Math department.

================================================================================
SECTION: B.S. in Data Science — Required Courses
================================================================================
FOUNDATION (18 credits):
  • DS 101 — Introduction to Data Science (3 cr)
  • CS 101 — Introduction to Programming (3 cr)
  • CS 150 — Discrete Mathematics for CS (3 cr)  [or MATH 300]
  • MATH 120 — Calculus I (4 cr)
  • CS 220 — Statistics for Data Science (3 cr)
  • MATH 230 — Linear Algebra (3 cr)  [Note: CS 260 may substitute with advisor approval]

CORE (15 credits):
  • DS 201 — Data Wrangling and Visualization (3 cr)
  • CS 250 — Data Structures and Algorithms (4 cr)
  • CS 310 — Database Systems (3 cr)
  • DS 301 — Applied Machine Learning (3 cr)
  • CS 301 — Software Engineering (3 cr)

ADVANCED (6 credits — choose 2):
  • CS 320 — Machine Learning (3 cr)
  • CS 430 — Natural Language Processing (3 cr)
  • CS 410 — Advanced Database Systems (3 cr)
  • CS 420 — Deep Learning (3 cr)

CAPSTONE:
  • DS 401 — Capstone in Data Science (3 cr)

ELECTIVES: 12 credits from approved list
GENERAL EDUCATION: Same as university-wide requirements (approximately 36 cr)
